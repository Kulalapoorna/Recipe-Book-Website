COCONUT CURRY

"Coconut Curry: A tropical delight that brings the creamy richness of coconut and the warmth of spices to your plate."

Coconut Curry

Ingredients:

- 400 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
- 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
- Salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. In a large pan, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes soft and translucent.
2. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for a couple of minutes until fragrant.
3. Add the curry powder, turmeric, cayenne pepper, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the spices release their aroma.
4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.
5. Pour in the coconut milk and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the curry thickens.
6. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
7. Serve your Coconut Curry hot with steamed rice or naan.

Indulge in the tropical flavors of your homemade Coconut Curry!
