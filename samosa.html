SAMOSA

"Samosa: A deliciously crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas, perfect for snacking."

Samosa

Ingredients:

- 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
- 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
- 1/2 teaspoon ajwain seeds (carom seeds)
- A pinch of salt
- For the filling:
- 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
- 1/2 cup green peas, boiled
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
- 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
- A handful of fresh coriander leaves, chopped
- Salt to taste
- Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, ghee, ajwain seeds, and a pinch of salt.
2. Add water little by little and knead the dough until it's smooth and firm. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
3. In a separate pan, heat some oil and add cumin seeds. Let them sizzle.
4. Add the mashed potatoes, boiled green peas, and all the spices. Cook for a few minutes until the filling is well-spiced and mixed.
5. Add the chopped coriander leaves and let the filling cool.
6. Divide the dough into small equal-sized portions and roll each portion into a ball.
7. Roll out each ball into a thin oval shape and cut it in half.
8. Create a cone shape from each half and stuff it with the prepared filling.
9. Seal the edges using a little water to form a triangular pastry.
10. Heat oil for deep frying and fry the samosas until they are golden brown and crispy.
11. Serve your Samosas hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce.

Indulge in the delicious crunch of homemade Samosas!
