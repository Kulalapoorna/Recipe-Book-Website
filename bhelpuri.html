BHEL PURI

"Bhel Puri: A delightful and tangy Indian street snack that tantalizes your taste buds with a mix of crispy, sweet, and spicy flavors."

Bhel Puri

Ingredients:

- 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
- 1/2 cup sev (crispy gram flour noodles)
- 1/2 cup boiled potatoes, chopped
- 1/2 cup onions, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup tomatoes, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup raw mango, finely chopped
- 2-3 tablespoons tamarind chutney
- 1-2 tablespoons green chutney
- 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
- A handful of fresh coriander leaves, chopped
- Salt to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the puffed rice, sev, boiled potatoes, chopped onions, tomatoes, and raw mango.
2. Add tamarind chutney and green chutney according to your taste preferences.
3. Sprinkle chaat masala, red chili powder, and salt over the mixture.
4. Gently toss and mix all the ingredients until they are well combined.
5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
6. Serve your Bhel Puri immediately as a delightful street snack.

Enjoy the tangy and crispy flavors of homemade Bhel Puri!
