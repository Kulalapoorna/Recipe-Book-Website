"Chicken Masala Curry: A tantalizing blend of spices that weaves a story of flavor in every succulent bite."

Chicken Masala Curry

Ingredients:

- 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 onions, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
- 1/2 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 2-3 green chilies, slit
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 tablespoon red chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
- 1 tablespoon coriander powder
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
2. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears.
4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Stir well and cook for a few minutes.
6. Now, add the chicken pieces and cook them until they turn white and are sealed on all sides.
7. Add the plain yogurt and mix well. Cook for a few more minutes until the chicken is coated with the yogurt.
8. Pour in a cup of water, reduce the heat, cover the pan, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the curry thickens.
9. Stir in the garam masala and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
11. Serve the Masala Chicken Curry hot with steamed rice, naan, or roti.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Masala Chicken Curry!
