About Us

Northern Nawabi is your passport to a world of culinary delights from the northern regions of India. Our recipes are a blend of traditional Nawabi flavors and modern twists, designed to satisfy your taste buds and inspire your inner chef. We are on a mission to make cooking enjoyable and accessible to everyone, from kitchen novices to seasoned cooks.

Our team, led by Simran Kaur, is dedicated to bringing you the best of Northern Indian cuisine. Whether you're craving biryanis, kebabs, rich curries, or delectable desserts, Northern Nawabi has you covered.
We provide step-by-step recipes, cooking tips, and a community of food enthusiasts to support your culinary journey.
