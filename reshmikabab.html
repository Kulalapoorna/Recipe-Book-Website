"Reshmi Kabab: Where tender whispers of spice dance on silk-smooth skewers."

Reshmi Kabab

Ingredients:

- 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into cubes
- 1/4 cup hung yogurt (strained yogurt)
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 2 tablespoons cashew paste
- 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
- 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
- 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
- Salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 4-5 wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, cream, cashew paste, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, and lemon juice.
2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them evenly. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight in the refrigerator.
3. Preheat your grill or barbecue to medium-high heat. If you don't have a grill, you can also use an oven broiler.
4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto the wooden skewers.
5. Brush the chicken skewers with vegetable oil to prevent sticking.
6. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a beautiful char on the outside. You can also cook them in the oven at 200°C (400°F) for about 20-25 minutes, turning once.
7. Once cooked, serve the Reshmi Kababs hot with naan, rice, or your favorite dipping sauce.

Enjoy!
