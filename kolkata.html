KOLKATA BIRIYANI

"Kolkata Biryani: Where tradition and innovation come together, creating a fragrant masterpiece in every plate."

Kolkata Biriyani

Ingredients:

For the rice:

•2 cups Basmati rice
•4-5 cups of water
•A few whole spices (bay leaves, cardamom, cloves)
•Salt to taste

For the meat:

•500g mutton or chicken
•2 large onions, thinly sliced
•2 tomatoes, chopped
•1/2 cup plain yogurt
•2-3 green chilies, slit
•2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
•1-inch piece of ginger, minced
•A handful of fresh mint leaves
•A handful of fresh coriander leaves
•2-3 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
•Ground spices (turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala)
•Saffron strands soaked in warm milk for coloring and flavor
•Sugar (to taste)
•Salt to taste

Instructions:

1.Wash and soak the Basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
2.In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add the soaked rice along with the whole spices and salt.
Cook until the rice is partially done (it should still have a bite). Drain and set aside.
3.In a separate pan, heat oil or ghee over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and cook until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove some for garnishing.
4.Add the minced ginger, garlic, green chilies, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil separates.
5.Add your choice of meat and cook until it changes color.
6.Add yogurt, mint leaves, and half of the coriander leaves. Stir well and cook for a few minutes.
7.Sprinkle ground spices (turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala), salt, and sugar. Mix well.
8.Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat mixture.
9.Place a layer of saffron strands soaked in warm milk for color and flavor.
10.Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal it with dough to trap steam.
11.Cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and the meat is tender.
12.Gently fluff the biryani with a fork, garnish with fried onions and the remaining coriander leaves, and serve hot.

Kolkata Biryani is known for its subtle sweetness and aromatic flavors. Enjoy this delightful dish!
